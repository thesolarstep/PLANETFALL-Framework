# PLANETFALL Philosophy: Calibrated Skepticism

## The Adversarial Mindset
PLANETFALL was born from the failure of trust. The core realization driving this project is that in the search for exoplanets, nature and instruments are often "lying" to us. Most "periodic signals" are not planets; they are astrophysical false positives or instrumental artifacts.

## Guilty Until Proven Innocent
In standard pipelines, the goal is often to find as many candidates as possible. PLANETFALL reverses this. 

1. **Every candidate is a False Positive.**
2. The framework acts as a **Prosecutor**, actively seeking evidence to "execute" (reject) the candidate.
3. Only the signals that survive this intense adversarial assault are deemed worthy of further investigation.

## Accuracy Over Quantity
We believe that missing a real discovery is a minor scientific setback, but publishing a false discovery is a major scientific failure. PLANETFALL is optimized to minimize False Positives at all costs.
